## Button States

### "Generate Now" (Blue/Cyan)

- Module is ready to generate
- Previous modules are complete
- Click to start generation

### "Generating..." (Cyan, disabled)

- Module is currently generating
- Cannot click
- Wait 2-5 minutes or refresh

### "View Details" (Green)

- Module is complete
- Click to see lesson plans and PPT decks
- Can view/download content

### "Locked" (Gray, disabled)

- Module cannot be generated yet
- Previous modules must be completed first
- Cannot click
